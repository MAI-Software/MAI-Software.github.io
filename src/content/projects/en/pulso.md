## Context

Pulso is an **example demo**: it shows how a SaaS product is presented inside the portfolio. Its content is fictional and will be replaced by real projects.

## Problem / opportunity

Logging work routines usually requires heavy tools. The idea explored: a minimal panel you understand at first glance, working with zero configuration.

## Solution

A single-screen interface: quick logging, weekly visualisation and simple export. No accounts, no friction.

## Outcome / status

Browsable demo in development. This page exists purely as a demonstration of the project template.
