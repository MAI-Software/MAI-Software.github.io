## Context

Nébula Runner is an **internal example concept**: it exists to show how a video game is presented inside the MAI Softwares portfolio. Its content is fictional and will be replaced by real projects.

## Problem / opportunity

Mobile runners usually rely on pure reflexes. The opportunity explored here: syncing obstacles with music so the player "feels" the level before seeing it.

## Solution

A prototype where each track generates its own obstacle layout from the rhythm. Short sessions, one-finger control and clear visual reading.

## Outcome / status

Concept stage. This page exists purely as a demonstration of the project template.
