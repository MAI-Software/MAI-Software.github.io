## Context

Atelier Norte is an **internal example concept**: it shows how a web project is presented inside the portfolio. The studio and its content are fictional.

## Problem / opportunity

Many creative studio websites prioritise effect over reading. The opportunity explored: an editorial site where work is presented as a publication, not a catalogue.

## Solution

Strong typographic composition, large imagery and minimal navigation. Static, fast and easy to extend.

## Outcome / status

Concept stage. This page exists purely as a demonstration of the project template.
